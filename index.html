Compound interest is interest calculated on both the principal and accumulated interest. The EMI calculator uses the standard reducing-balance formula: EMI = P × r × (1+r)ⁿ / ((1+r)ⁿ - 1), where P = principal, r = monthly rate, n = tenure in months. Each month, interest is charged only on the outstanding balance, which reduces as you pay EMIs.
